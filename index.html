I have played games for a few years now, mostly on my desktop and phone. The games I usually play come with customization features. This could be through character creation, skillsets, or gear. The genres I normally go for include action, mmorpg, fantasy, adventure, free-to-play, gacha, single-player, and obviously character customization. I have also tried shooters, survival and horror, fighting, and rhythm. Although I have tried a variety of games, I am a casual gamer who does not compete with others. I merely take my time and enjoy exploring the gaming worlds through single-player modes. Below are five of the games I am currently playing.

Aliens: Fireteam Elite

Enter a world where a mysterious pathogen has devastating effects on lifeforms on the planet you are exploring. Select your class and customize your weapons and perks to take on all xenomorph and pathogen mutation threats and do everything you can to survive the pathogen hive. This is a third-person survival shooter game where you can run challenge cards and play several game modes with your friends.

In an Internet world called Deep Log, there is an error that causes malfunctions in game data. Play as hunters and equip them with weapons, cards, and chips to defeat enemies with irregular data through various stages of the classic world of Mega Man X. This is a free-to-play side-scrolling shooting game where you can play solo or coop.

Monster Super League is a free-to-play laidback gacha game where you can manage resources, explore different regions, and capture Astromons. There are over 600 unique Astromons, including evolutions, and different ways to gear them. Equip them with gems, trinkets, and skillbooks to compete in the Astromon League and clan battles as well as take on a variety of dungeons.
